Aomori Winter Festival

The theme of the festival is “Let’s play with snow.” There is a huge snow slide, and many recreational activities for family and friends. Winter festival to enjoy Aomori’s freezing season Enjoy snow arts such as snow sculptures and winter fireworks that can taste in only this season. * Events schedules are subject to change without notice due to the weather.

Time: Early February

Address: 2-chome, Gappo, Aomori-shi, Aomori 030-0902
