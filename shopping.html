A-FACTORY

A-FACTORY is a duty-free market-style shopping center selling local products such as sweets, processed goods, fresh vegetables and fruits. The most featured facility in A-FACTORY is the apple cidre (cider) factory which makes cidre and juices using Aomori grown apples. The production process of our cidre can be observed through the glassed windows at the factory. Tasting is also available with purchase of a tasting card.

Address: 1-4-2 Yanagikawa Aomori-shi Aomori 038-0012

Nearest station : JR Aomori Station


Tsugaru-han Neputa Mura

There is a facility for visitors to see, experience, and buy Tsugaru traditional artwork, with shops selling local produce such as vegetables, apples, and rice.

Address: 361 Kamenoko-machi Hirosaki-shi Aomori 036-8332

Nearest station : JJR Hirosaki Station Central Entrance Bus Stop
