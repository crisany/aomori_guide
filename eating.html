Restaurants

Sanfuri-Yokocho

Here you’ll find an abundance of small, Showa-style food stalls all stood in a row underneath hanging paper lanterns, creating a feeling of the good old days. The mountain and marine delicacies of Aomori, as well as conversation with the locals can be enjoyed here.

Address: 3 Chome-8-3 Honcho, Aomori, 030-0802, Japan

Nearest station : JR Aomori Station


Yu-Sa Asamushi

At this roadside rest area you will find a hot spring with a stunning view of the sun setting on Mutsu Bay. Try out their original box lunch, ‘Asaben’, or cassis (blackcurrant) soft-serve ice cream.

Address: 341-19, Aza Hotarutani, Asamushi, Aomori city

Nearest station : JR Aomori Station
